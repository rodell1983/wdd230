Target Audience

Sunnyville Chamber of Commerce will target two personas. The first group is small business owners. Typically, these are middle age, middle to upper-middle class small business owners. Many of them own only one establishment. They want to get their business recognized and are interested in advertisement and sponsorship to do so. The second group is local citizens that are interested in supporting the community. This group Consist of older citizens who like local "mom and pop" establishments and prefer to purchase locally without sending their money overseas. Because of their age, generally they are less tech savvy and prefer easy to navigate websites with few distractions. They mostly use home desktops with the occasional cell phone browsing.
