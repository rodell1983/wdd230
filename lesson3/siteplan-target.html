Target Audience

Persona One - Business Owner

Bob

Demographics

  • Middle Age
  • Single Business Owner

Goals

  • Wants recognition for his business
  • Looking to advertise
  • Willing to sponsor local youth teams

Environment

  • Comfortable using mobile devices
  • Has access to highspeed internet
  • Fairly Tech Savvy

Persona Two - Local Resident

Jill

Demographics

  • Senior Citizen
  • Retired

Goals

  • Prefers to shop locally
  • Looking for discounts
  • Like to be able to talk to the business owner in person

Environment

  • Generally uses desktop computers
  • Uses slower internet connections
  • Not Tech Savvy
  • Likes easy to navigate web pages

Scenarios

  • The site will allow for members to post advertisements and discount.
  • The site will have a form page to sign up as a new member.
  • Appearance and navigation will adapt to multiple devices and screen size
