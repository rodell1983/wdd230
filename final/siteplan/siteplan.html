Site Name

Temple Inn & Suites

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote the Temple Inn & Suits. We provide logging near all the temples of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. We offer safe and affordable logging to meet your needs. Long-term stay for missionaries, family history library, daycare and much more. On this site you will be able to explore temples, make reservations, and schedule multiple services such as receptions.

Target Audience & Scenarios

My target audience will include the following

  1. Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints Members
  2. Missionaries
  3. Vacationers who want a family friendly atmosphere

Scenarios will include the following

  • The site will provide information on several temples.
  • Users can explore the different hotel amenities and book rooms
  • Users can book wedding receptions
  • Discounts offered based on the group size
  • Allow users to create a like list of temples
